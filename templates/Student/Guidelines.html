Student Guidelines & Code of Conduct

Adherence to these rules is mandatory for all students.

1

Academic Attendance

A minimum of 75% academic attendance is mandatory for all students in every course. Failure to meet this requirement may result in being barred from sitting for final examinations.

2

Emergency Leave Procedure

In case of a genuine medical or family emergency requiring an immediate outpass or leave, students can directly contact the on-duty Caretaker. This provision is for verified emergencies only.

3

Parental Permission for Leave

You must have parents' permission for any planned outpass or leave. This must be obtained through the official channels before leaving the campus.

4

Campus Reporting Time

You must report back to campus before the given time. Late entries will be documented and may lead to disciplinary action.

5

Safety Off-Campus

The Administration is not accountable for your safety after you leave the campus premises. You are expected to conduct yourself responsibly at all times.

6

Student Identification

Students must carry their official ID card at all times on campus and present it upon request by any staff or security personnel.

7

Anti-Ragging Policy

Ragging in any form is a serious criminal offense and is strictly prohibited. Any student found engaging in or supporting ragging will face immediate suspension and legal action.

8

General Conduct

Vandalism, possession of prohibited substances, and disruptive behavior are strictly forbidden and will result in severe disciplinary measures.
